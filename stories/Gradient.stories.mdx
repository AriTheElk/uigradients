# Gradient

The de-facto drop-in component for all your gradient needs.

## Overview

- If you do not wrap your components in a `<GradientProvider/>`, the default presets will be used.
- You do not need more than one `<GradientProvider/>`. You can wrap your entire app in one and the presets will be available to all your instances of `<Gradient/>`.
- You can nest `<GradientProvider/>` if you need more presets to be available to only specific parts of your app.
- If you do not specify a `preset` on the `<Gradient/>`, a random preset will be used.

## Text Masking

Text masking allows you to create gradient colored text. It gracefully falls back on older browsers that don't support gradient text.
